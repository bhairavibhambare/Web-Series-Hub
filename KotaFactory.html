About the Series

Kota Factory is an Indian web series created by TVF (The Viral Fever). It shows the life of students who come to Kota, Rajasthan to prepare for competitive exams like IIT-JEE. The story follows Vaibhav Pandey, a determined student, and his experiences with friendship, pressure, and hope. The series is famous for being shot in black and white, symbolizing the struggles and dreams of students.
