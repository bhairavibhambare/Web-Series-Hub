About the Series

Mirzapur is one of India’s most popular action-crime thriller web series, streaming on Amazon Prime Video. It is set in the crime-infested city of Mirzapur in Uttar Pradesh, where law and order are dictated by the gun. The story revolves around the powerful mafia don Akhandanand Tripathi, also known as Kaleen Bhaiya, who runs both a carpet business and an illegal arms empire. His son, Munna Tripathi, is short-tempered and ambitious, eager to prove himself as the heir to his father’s throne. Two young brothers, Guddu and Bablu Pandit, enter the world of crime after being lured by the Tripathis’ power and wealth. As they rise within the ranks, greed and rivalry ignite a violent war for dominance. The peaceful lives of the Pandit brothers quickly spiral into chaos and bloodshed. The series brilliantly portrays the corruption and brutality that exist in small-town politics and organized crime. Each character is layered with ambition, revenge, and emotional depth, making the story unpredictable and gripping. The intense dialogues and raw realism give Mirzapur its signature dark tone. .

The series is packed with intense drama, sharp dialogues, and unforgettable performances that make it one of the most talked-about Indian web shows.

Main Characters

Kaleen Bhaiya (Akhandanand Tripathi)

The don of Mirzapur and the ruler of its criminal empire. A calm, calculating leader who believes in control and legacy.

Guddu Pandit

A muscle-bound youth drawn into crime by ambition and revenge. His transformation is one of the show’s most striking arcs.

Bablu Pandit

Guddu’s younger brother — intelligent, strategic, and rational. He initially tries to keep order but is slowly consumed by violence.

Munna Tripathi

Kaleen Bhaiya’s son, impulsive and power-hungry. His reckless behavior often leads to catastrophic results.

Beena Tripathi

The intelligent and cunning wife of Kaleen Bhaiya. She plays her own dangerous game to survive in the Tripathi household.

Golu Gupta

A bright and bold student who becomes a key player in Mirzapur’s power battle after the tragic events of Season 1.

Seasons Overview

Season 1 (2018): Introduces the violent world of Mirzapur, focusing on the rise of Guddu and Bablu Pandit. It ends in tragedy, setting up a powerful revenge narrative.

Season 2 (2020): The story intensifies as new alliances form and revenge becomes the central theme. The power struggle escalates between the Tripathis and the Pandits.

Season 3 (2024):after the explosive events of Season 2, with new power struggles and betrayals shaping the future of Mirzapur. The fight for the throne intensifies as old enemies return and new alliances form, promising even more action, revenge, and chaos..
